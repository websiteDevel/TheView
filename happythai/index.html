ABOUT THE VIEW

The View is a Classic rooftop sky bar right in the heart of Quy Nhon city, Binh Dinh province. The open air terrace offers mesmerizing views, so whatever the mood and whatever the weather, The View most likely will have everything to offer. A great spot for both relaxing cocktails early evening with some friends, as well as mouth watering food for a more vibrant party atmosphere later at night.
